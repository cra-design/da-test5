Ways to contact the CRA

Contact the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) if you have questions or need help with your CRA account:

Online

Use your CRA My Account to:

  • Update your personal information, including your address and your marital status
  • View and update information about children in your care
  • Apply for and view Canada Child Benefit (CCB) payments
